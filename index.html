HOW TO MAKE A BRAND BOARD

Completion time: 30 Minutes
Cool rating: 20% Cooler

Are you struggling to determine how you want your brand to look? Make a brand board! Here are a few easy steps to help you get started.

  • THINGS YOU'LL NEED

  • a computer
  • a design program
  • a creative mind

  1. START FORMING IDEAS!

    There’s different ways that people brainstorm, choose what is best for you! This brainstorming will have to include the brand's purpose, target audience, and aesthetics.

  2. PICK YOUR PALETTE!

    Pick a few colors that go with your brand aesthetic. There should be a primary color, secondary color, and some supporting colors.

    Colors and their shades.

  3. SELECT SOME FONTS!

    The fonts you choose are important for your brand! This includes a primary, secondary, and accent.

  4. ADD SOME VISUALS!

    Add some patterns, motifs, and textures that all represent your brand.

  5. FIND YOUR IMAGERY!

    Add photos and design examples (possibly some mockups) about how your brand will potentially look.

  6. COMBINE YOUR IDEAS!

    Bring all your elements together! Colors, fonts, visuals, and imagery - ontoone clean layout for your brand.

    Elements of a brand board all together.
